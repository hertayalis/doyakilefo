In the photo, Biel is seen covering her gorgeous tulle bridal gown by Giambattista Valli with a white-and-black robe. Next to her, Timberlake looks cool in a beige suit and hat, white dress shirt, and black shoes.

Biel and Timberlake married on October 19, 2012, in Puglia, Italy. The ceremony took place at the stunning Borgo Egnazia resort with views of the Roman ruins and the Mediterranean Sea.
