Who is Patti Stefani?

Patti Stefani is an Irish-American woman who previously worked as an accountant before becoming a housewife. Furthermore, she is best known as Gwen Stefani’s mother, as well as a co-founder, lead vocalist, and primary songwriter of the band No Doubt, whose singles include “Just a Girl,” “Spiderwebs,” and “Don’t Speak” from their 1995 breakthrough studio album Tragic Kingdom, as well as “Hey Baby” and “It’s My Life” from later albums.
