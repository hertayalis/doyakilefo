Who Are Ivant Fields And Gina Tobey?

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Justin Fields, the American football quarterback for the Chicago Bears, was raised in Kennesaw, Georgia, by his parents, Ivant Fields and Gina Tobey, who are now divorced.

Despite their separation, both parents played a significant role in Justin’s football career.

In particular, his mother, Gina, advised him to attend the University of Georgia for college due to their outstanding football program, which ultimately helped him in his career.

Justin Skyler Fields is an American football quarterback currently playing for the Chicago Bears in the NFL. In 2021, the Chicago Bears chose Justin in the first round of the 2021 NFL Draft. 

Fields initially attended the University of Georgia, and as a freshman, he served as the backup to starting quarterback Jake Fromm. 

He was later transferred to Ohio State University for his college football career.

At Ohio State, he achieved recognition as the Graham–George Offensive Player of the Year twice and reached the 2021 College Football Playoff National Championship.

Justin Fields Parents

Justin Fields is a young football player who was born on March 5, 1999, to his parents, Ivant Fields and Gina Tobey.

He has two sisters, Jaiden and Jessica Fields. Throughout his journey as a football player, Justin has received support from his family, especially his parents, who have played active roles in assisting him.

Justin’s Father Ivant Fields 

Justin’s father, Ivant Pablo Fields, is a former police officer of Atlanta who served in the Atlanta Police Department from 1990 to 2012.

Prior to his police career, Ivant had a career in football. He played for Eastern Kentucky University as a linebacker.

However, he did not reach the NFL as a player and later chose to transition into a police officer.

With a background in football, Ivant somehow influenced his son Justin to pursue a career in football.

Ivant Fields and Gina divorced when Justin was still young. Despite the divorce, Ivant remained a pillar of strength in Justin’s life and was highly involved in raising him.

After his divorce, he married Jo Ann Claudrick Fields, a lawyer who has also been supportive of Justin.

Similarly, Justin’s mother, Gina Tobey, has guided Justin on his path to becoming a football player.

She advised Justin to choose the University of Georgia for his college education, recognizing the university’s strong football program as a crucial step toward his career goals. 

With the love and support of his family, especially his parents, Justin Fields has been able to become a successful football player and pursue his dreams.

Sister Jaden And Jessica Are Also Athlete

Justin Fields has two stepsisters, Jaden and Jessica, who are both athletes and are involved in different sports.

Jaden is a graduate of Grady College of Journalism and Mass Communication. She is into softball. 

During her high school softball career, Jaiden received significant recognition. She earned the title of Player of the Year in 2018 and was named to the all-state team for three consecutive years.

Beyond her achievements in sports, Jaiden has ventured into the world of entertainment. She signed a Name, Image, and Likeness (NIL) deal with WWE as part of their “Next in Line” program.

Justin Fields’ youngest sister, Jessica, is another talented athlete in the family. Jessica has pursued basketball and softball as her sports of choice.

Jessica’s athletic prowess was evident from a young age. She was honored with the title of Most Valuable Player (MVP) during her 6th-grade year at the CCJBC (Cherokee County Junior Basketball Conference) tournament.
