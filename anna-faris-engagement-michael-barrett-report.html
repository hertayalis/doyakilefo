Anna Faris Might Be Engaged to Cinematographer Michael Barrett

May 2024 ¬∑ 2 minute read
  • Actress and podcaster Anna Faris reportedly confirmed her engagement to cinematographer Michael Barrett.
  • Faris has been linked to Barrett since 2017, following her separation from now ex-husband Chris Pratt.
  • According to E! News, Faris was spotted wearing what looked like an engagement ring and appeared to verbally confirm the news.

Another celebrity wedding could be on the horizon.

E! News reported that Anna Faris appeared to confirm her engagement to her boyfriend of two years, cinematographer Michael Barrett.

Per the outlet, Faris and Barrett were photographed over the weekend, when members of the paparazzi asked, "How are you guys?" Faris apparently simply replied, "We're wonderful." When the photographers then said, "Congratulations," Faris reportedly replied, "Thank you." And that's not all. When asked if they were ready for their "big day," Faris said "no," suggesting that a wedding is in the works.

It was first reported that Faris was dating Barrett in October 2017. At the time, People reported, "Anna has been hanging out with Michael for weeks," after meeting him during the production of Overboard. People's source continued, "They were hanging out occasionally in September, but started seeing each other more frequently recently."

Faris' ex-husband, Jurassic World actor Chris Pratt, married Katherine Schwarzenegger earlier this year in June, after announcing their engagement in January.
