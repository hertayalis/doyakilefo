Understanding Taylor Lautner

As an American actor, Taylor Lautner is widely recognized for his portrayal of Jacob Black, a werewolf, in the renowned Twilight Saga movie series from 2008 to 2012.

Born on February 11, 1992, in Grand Rapids, Michigan, Lautner commenced his acting career at a young age, featuring in commercials and TV shows. Notably, he gained prominence with significant roles in movies like Cheaper by the Dozen 2 and The Adventures of Sharkboy and Lavagirl in 3-D.

Subsequently, Lautner’s role as Jacob Black in the Twilight saga propelled him to international stardom. Post Twilight, he appeared in several films such as Abduction, Grown Ups 2, and Run the Tide, along with television appearances in Cuckoo and Scream Queens. In 2022, Lautner tied the knot with Taylor Dome and co-hosts a podcast named The Squeeze.
