David Arquette and Wife Christina McLarty Welcome Baby No. 2

June 2024 · 2 minute read

Congrats are in order for David Arquette and his wife, Christina McLarty!

The couple welcomed their second baby, a son named Augustus Alexis Arquette, on Wednesday, March 8, David and Christina announced via Instagram.

"Augustus Alexis Arquette. March 8, 2017," the 45-year-old actor captioned an adorable, close-up pic of his newborn son on the photo-sharing site. Christina, 35, similarly shared a black and white snapshot of the little guy's tiny hand with the same caption.

Augustus will reportedly go by the nickname Gus. His middle name is a sweet tribute to David's late sister, actress Alexis Arquette, who sadly passed away on Sept. 11, 2016.

The Never Been Kissed star and his actress wife previously announced they were expecting Baby No. 2 last October. The couple — who tied the knot in April 2015 — are also parents to a two-year-old son, Charlie West Arquette, who was born in April 2014.
