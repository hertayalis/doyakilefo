Anne Hathaway reveals the reason for her weight gain and hits back at fat shamers

July 2024 · 2 minute read

The Oscar-winning actress pre-empted body shaming in an Instagram post

    Anne Hathaway has got one step ahead of body shamers by explaining the reason for her recent weight gain. actress has been gaining weight via intense bodybuilding workouts, and admitted she was preparing for people to comment on her changing figure over the coming weeks.

    Sharing a time-lapse video of her gym routine, which saw her training with resistance bands and weights, Anne told her 10.4 million followers: "I am gaining weight for a movie role and it is going well. To all the people who are going to fat shame me in the upcoming months, it's not me, it's you. Peace xx." She added: "PS – I wanted to set this to Queen's 'Fat Bottomed Girls' but copyright said no. Continued Peace."

    Anne Hathaway is gaining weight for a new film role

    Anne's post was praised by her fans, who complimented her on her changing physique: "Whatever your weight you're still gorgeous," one person commented, while another wrote: "Isn't it a terrible world where someone has to clarify to strangers (or anyone) why their body is changing?"
