Cyan Boujee Social Media

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Cyan Boujee Social Media: Cyan Boujee, officially known as Honour Zuma Zacn is a well-known figure in South Africa.

She’s a model, disc jockey, brand ambassador, social media influencer, YouTuber, Instagram sensation, content creator, make-up artist, and philanthropist.

Boujee is famous for her social media content and boasts a massive following across several platforms where she also shares captivating photos.

She initially started as a makeup artist and began posting tutorials and brand content as her fame and following grew on social media.

On her social media platforms, Boujee also shares lifestyle videos of herself having fun, living her best life, and showcasing her modeling ventures.

While living her best life, she founded the “Reaching Out Foundation”, a charitable initiative aimed at assisting the poor and the needy.

Though Boujee hasn’t made a massive name as a DJ, she has performed on several stages and earned a reputation as a DJ in the country.

Launching her career as a content creator and YouTuber, Boujee established her self-titled YouTube channel on December 10, 2019.

To diversify her content, she introduced the “Boujee Space” YouTube page in 2022, focusing on makeup tutorials and beauty tips.

As of Wednesday, August 9, 2023, her YouTube page, Boujee Space has over 84,000 subscribers and an impressive view count of 3,731,793.

Apart from YouTube, she has impressive numbers across other social media platforms, amassing over 430,000 followers on Instagram alone.

As a businesswoman, Boujee has done promotional posts for popular brands, including Umbrobo, Limits, and Clere, among others.

She’s a brand ambassador for Fashion Nova and also has a collaborative deal with the online shoe store, All Brand Sneakers.

In August 2023, Cyan Boujee hit the headlines again, and this time for a less desirable reason as her explicit sex tape hits online.

Boujee was recently engaged in an adult activity with an unidentified male who may have leaked the private moment between the two to the public.

Social media trolls immediately turned up the viral spread of the explicit video, which may have been published without the consent of Cyan Boujee.

At this time of writing (Wednesday, August 9, 2023), the DJ had not yet responded to the viral video.

Cyan Boujee Social Media

Cyan Boujee owns a Twitter account with over 127,000 followers, a TikTok account with over 467,000 followers, and an Instagram account with over 430,000 followers. The South African DJ is very active on these social media platforms.
